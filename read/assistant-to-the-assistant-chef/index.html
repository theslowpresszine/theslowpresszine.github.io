Assistant to the Assistant Chef

Assistant to the Assistant Chef is a non-hierarchical interview zine by The Slow Press featuring people who cook professionally, from stage to sous. Featuring the romantic, the mundane, the in-and-outs, the down-and-outs - this zine’s for the curious readers who want to know whether people really do say “yes chef.”

Featuring:

  • Chia Yong Jin
  • Panida (Kat) Rojanapiensatith
  • Ellen Nathania
  • Isabel Galiñanes García
  • Joseph Ong

A5 (125 x 210mm), 88 pages, self-published and printed. Launched in Singapore, November 2025 with an offset print of 500 copies.
